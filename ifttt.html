Business case

IFTTT (IF This Then That) savings.

Value Proposition

A new and funny way to save money.

Targets

People who struggle to save money.

Problem

We've been saving money the same way for decades: a monthly recurrent payment towards a saving account. Let's make it funnier and turn anything we do in money saving. Reward yourself or punish yourself for bad habits.

Solution

Link our banking App with others thanks to IFTTT.
examples:
· save every time it rains (works with Weather App)
· save when you hit your fitness goal (works with Runkeeper)
· save when F91 Dudelange scores a goal (Works with ESPN)
